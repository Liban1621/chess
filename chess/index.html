    • What is CHESS?
    Chess is a classic two-player strategy game with 64 squares on an 8x8 grid. Each player has 16 pieces, including a king, queen, rooks, knights, bishops, and pawns. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king, forcing it into a position where it can't escape capture. Chess emphasizes tactics, strategy, and intellectual challenge.
